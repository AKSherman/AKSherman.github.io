SQL Data Exploration of Worldwide COVID Data from 2020-2021

In this project, I explored global COVID-19 data to derive insights on infection and mortality rates, along with vaccination progress across different countries and continents. Using SQL, I worked with two datasets: one focused on COVID-19 cases and deaths, and another on vaccinations. Key tasks included: -Analyzing Total Cases vs. Total Deaths: Calculated the death percentage relative to total cases across various locations, focusing on regions like the United States. -Infection Rates by Population: Identified countries with the highest COVID-19 infection rates by comparing total cases to population size. -Mortality Insights: Analyzed the countries and continents with the highest death counts and calculated the death rate by population. -Vaccination Analysis: Joined vaccination data with COVID case data to calculate the percentage of the population vaccinated and tracked the progress over time. -Visualization Preparation: Created views for key metrics such as the highest infection rates, death counts, and vaccination rates to facilitate future data visualization. These analyses provided a detailed understanding of the pandemic's impact globally and helped uncover key trends in infection and mortality rates, as well as vaccination rollout.
